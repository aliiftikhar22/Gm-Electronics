Our Story

From an electronics business to a dedicated appliance branch

The business behind GM Electronics has been trading in electronics since 1992. Over three decades, it built a reputation on straightforward dealing and stock that customers could rely on.

GM Electronics was created as a new, dedicated branch of that business — focused specifically on home appliances. Instead of trying to be everything to everyone, this branch concentrates on what households and shops actually need most: refrigerators, air conditioners, microwaves, ovens, and the everyday small appliances like irons, sandwich makers and exhaust fans.

We sell only — at both retail price for individual customers and wholesale price for bulk quantity buyers such as shopkeepers and contractors. We don't offer repair services, so our full attention stays on sourcing and selling appliances at fair prices.
